# WhitePapers-I-Read

This repository is grouped into months. Various papers I read or re-read in a particular month will be linked there. It is designed to keep all my findings and readings in one central location. 

Blog posts, newsletters and articles that are informative will also be added here. It will cover various concepts and ideas that interest or matter to me.

You are welcome to use this as your reference too. If you have knowledge of any papers you think may help me or anyone, feel free to reach out. Create an issue or fork or something. You can also reach out to me on my twitter or email.
